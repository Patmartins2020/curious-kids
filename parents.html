For Parents & Guardians

This page explains how Curious Kids Q&A works, how we protect your child’s privacy, and how you can guide them as they ask questions about God, life and feelings.

1. How the Question System Works

Children can send questions with or without their first name. We encourage parents to sit with them while they type.

  • No surnames, phone numbers or contact details are requested.
  • Public questions may appear on the Question Wall, but never with full identity.
  • Private / consultation questions are kept hidden and seen only by the admin for personal responses.

2. Safety & Privacy

We are building this as a faith-based, child-safe space. Please read this together with the Legal page for full details.

  • We do not sell or trade children’s data.
  • All questions are stored locally in the child’s browser for now.
  • You can clear questions from the device at any time using the admin tools.

3. How You Can Use This Tool

  • Let kids type exactly how they feel, then review together.
  • Use the answers as a starting point for deeper family conversations.
  • Send your own “adult” questions via the Adult Forum if you need guidance on how to respond.

If you have concerns or suggestions, you can reach the project owner directly using the contact details that will be added here later.
